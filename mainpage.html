Problem

85% of plastic used for packaging ends up in landfills

Our mission is to promote and encourage environmental sustainability by incentivizing recycling through our app. We aim to make recycling a fun and rewarding experience for users by awarding them points for their recycling efforts. Our app will provide users with easy access to recycling information and resources, as well as opportunities to earn rewards and discounts from local businesses who support our cause. Through our app, we hope to inspire positive behavioral change and contribute to a cleaner, healthier planet for generations to come.
